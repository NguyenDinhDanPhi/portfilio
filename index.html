About Me

A mobile developer focused on delivering high-quality iOS apps.

Hello, my name is Nguyễn Đình Đan Phi, a Mobile Developer with over 1 year of experience in mobile app development. I specialize in using SwiftUI and UIKit to create smooth and user-friendly applications.

I have experience integrating third-party login methods like Google and Facebook, and I work with the MVVM architecture and popular design patterns such as Singleton, Delegate, ... to build scalable and maintainable code.

Additionally, I work with RESTful APIs, manage the app release process via TestFlight and App Store, and use SourceTree for version control, making it easier to track and manage changes effectively.

With a passion for technology, my goal is to become a professional iOS developer, always ready to take on new challenges and continuously improve to meet the growing demands of the mobile technology industry.
